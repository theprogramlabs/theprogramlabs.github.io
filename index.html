Advisor Service

Pain Points We Address

Strategic Confusion & Vision-to-Execution Gaps
  • Organizations moving from strategic ambiguity to clarity
  • Ms. Scrappy's Pain: "We need to build programs and grow organization"
  • Ms. Pro's Pain: "Our programs aren't delivering what we need"
Leadership & Organizational Alignment
  • Teams requiring consensus on core priorities
  • Leadership fatigue, burnout risk, and workload management
  • Learning to say no and manage expectations
Implementation & Execution Challenges
  • Bridging vision-to-action through concrete timelines
  • Price conscious organizations needing ROI in 6-12 months
  • Entrepreneurs seeking to "avoid common stumbling blocks that keep them stuck"

Our Methodology

Structured Coaching Framework
  • 20-Category Assessment: Covering Commitment, Execution, Mindset, Perception, and Strategy
  • 4-Step Process: Intake workbook → Discussion → Program Personality Test → Point out execution gaps
  • 3-Phase Framework: Assessment & Alignment → Program Design → Implementation Planning
  • Key Differentiator: Lean startup approach for nonprofits vs traditional consulting
